The Cardiology department is the Main site of healthcare of the hospital.

The department provides the best Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) output. The Neurology department is the main site for all nerve problems.

The neurology departmenet treats diseases related to the brain and spinal cord, Peripheral nerves and muscles. They work on the diseases suchh as epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis(MS), Parkinson's disease etc.
